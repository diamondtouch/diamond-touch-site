Ceramic Coating

From $799 sedan / $999 SUV
  • Full Signature detail included first
  • Full decontamination + clay bar + single-stage correction
  • IPA panel prep & ceramic coating application
  • 1–2 year hydrophobic protection

6–8 hours · Full detail + correction + ceramic protection

Everything Included

  • Full Signature detail (complete interior + exterior wash) — included first
  • Full decontamination wash
  • Clay bar decontamination
  • Single-stage paint correction
  • IPA panel prep wipe (ensures clean surface for coating)
  • Ceramic coating application
  • 1–2 year hydrophobic protection

What Makes This Different from Paint Correction

Adds a genuine ceramic coating on top of corrected paint — creating a semi-permanent hydrophobic layer that repels water, dirt, and UV rays for 1–2 years.

Best For
