We serve Murrieta, Temecula, Winchester, and Wildomar. We may accommodate nearby areas — contact us to confirm if your location is covered.

Service times vary by package and vehicle condition. Interior-only takes 2–3 hours. Full details run 3–5 hours. Premium details including paint correction can take 5–8 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.

We bring all of our own equipment and products. All we need is access to an outdoor spigot (water source) and a standard 110V electrical outlet. We handle everything else.

The $25 deposit goes toward your total service cost and confirms your appointment slot. It helps us plan our schedule and ensures both your time and ours is protected. The remaining balance is due on the day of service.

Life happens. We ask for at least 24 hours notice to reschedule without losing your deposit. Contact us at sales@diamondtouchdetails.com and we'll find a new time that works for you.
